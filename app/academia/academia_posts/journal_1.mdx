## Abstract

The chapter delves into the application of artificial intelligence (AI) in bioinformatics and computational biology, emphasizing the revolutionary insights AI provides into complex biological systems. AI techniques such as machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and data mining are transforming the analysis of large biological datasets. These advancements enable significant breakthroughs in areas like drug discovery and personalized medicine. The chapter also discusses the importance of collaboration between computer scientists, biologists, and clinicians to unlock AI's full potential in biomedicine. It also addresses ethical challenges related to data privacy, patent laws, and bioethics, stressing the importance of ongoing research and interdisciplinary partnerships for future AI innovations in healthcare.
