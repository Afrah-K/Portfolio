## Abstract

The paper presents a study on detecting emotions through sentiment analysis on social media platforms, with a focus on Twitter. With the increasing number of posts shared online, users express their opinions and feelings on various topics. This research uses machine learning models to extract and analyze the emotions behind user tweets on specific subjects. By categorizing posts into different emotional categories, this approach provides insights into user perspectives on key topics.
